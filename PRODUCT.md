# Product

## Register

product

## Users

PromptPal serves internal knowledge workers handling project and customer information. They use the
product to complete focused AI-assisted tasks such as generation, chat review, transcription, and
usage analysis. Transcribe users need to turn local audio, video, or live microphone input into an
editable, searchable, exportable transcript without sending the source media to a processing server.

## Product Purpose

PromptPal provides a secure, trustworthy workbench for repeated AI-assisted tasks. The Transcribe
surface prioritizes fast entry into local transcription, understandable processing status, durable
browser-local history, and efficient review and export of completed transcripts.

## Brand Personality

Calm, trustworthy, and practical. The product should feel familiar and focused, with enough warmth
to avoid an austere enterprise-tool character.

## Anti-references

- Generic AI wrappers built around purple gradients, decorative glow, or glass panels.
- Over-carded dashboards that obscure the primary task.
- Novel navigation or control patterns that make standard actions harder to recognize.
- Decorative motion or saturated inactive controls.

## Design Principles

1. Put the active work first and keep product chrome quiet.
2. Make local processing, storage, and runtime behavior understandable.
3. Prefer familiar navigation and controls so users can stay in flow.
4. Preserve user work across refreshes and make recovery paths explicit.
5. Use restrained visual hierarchy, reserving emphasis for commands and state.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA for contrast, keyboard access, focus visibility, and semantic labeling. Respect
reduced-motion preferences and never rely on color alone to communicate state.
